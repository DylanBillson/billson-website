This month with Galea: foundations, clarity, and the next steps in our assessment tools.

Monthly update · December 2025

01 · Opening

Strengthening the foundations

This month, we’ve been focusing on strengthening the foundations of Galea, improving clarity, consistency, and the small details that make everyday use smoother. We want to share the work happening behind the scenes and a few insights from across the team.

02 · What we’re focusing on

Looking ahead to the next release

Over the coming month, we’ll be deepening our work on clarity, user guidance, and preparing the groundwork for the next major release. A key focus is helping practitioners compare their own areas of concern with the patterns seen across 250,000+ previous assessments.

03 · Department spotlight

This month’s focus: Development

This month, our Development Team has been reviewing phrasing across the assessment tool to ensure language aligns with the latest NICE guidance and practitioner feedback. These improvements will roll out gradually over the coming months.

From the team

“Clear, consistent language is one of the most powerful ways we can support good conversations between practitioners and the people they work with.”

04 · In practice

How Galea supports flexible conversations

Did you know? Many users aren’t aware that you can switch between assessment types at any stage. This makes it easier to broaden or narrow your focus depending on what emerges in conversation.

Waiting times continue to be one of the biggest challenges in UK mental-health care. As we refine Galea, we’re constantly thinking about how our tools can support services to make the best possible use of the time they do have with people.
