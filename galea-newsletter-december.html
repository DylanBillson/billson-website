This month with Galea: foundations, clarity, and the next steps in our assessment tools.

Monthly update · December 2025

01 · Opening

Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year

Welcome to our final newsletter of 2025. We’d like to start by wishing you a very merry Christmas and a happy New Year from everyone here at Galea.

Throughout December, we’ve continued working hard on platform improvements, particularly machine learning and the foundations that make Galea smoother and more intuitive to use. Below, you’ll find a look at what’s been happening behind the scenes, along with a few reflections from our team.

02 · What we’re focusing on

Looking ahead to 2026

This year has been a big one: we launched our rebrand to Galea, delivered the first stage of our improved UI, and built the background infrastructure that will power the next generation of features.

All of this work has been leading towards an even bigger year in 2026. We’re excited to share more soon and even more excited to get these improvements into your hands.

Did you know?

You can switch between assessment types at any stage. This makes it easier to broaden or narrow your focus depending on what emerges in conversation.

05 · Stay in touch

Help us shape what comes next

If you know someone who would benefit from these updates, please pass this newsletter along. The more practitioners and team members we hear from, the better we can shape Galea to meet real-world needs.

Galea is built with its users, not just for them. Whether you have suggestions, challenges to flag, ideas you’d like us to explore, or stories of where Galea has helped, we’d really value a conversation.
