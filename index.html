What is Xperfecta?

Xperfecta connects decision makers to expertise from anywhere at any time.

Accessible Intelligence

Everyone should be able to access Artificial Intelligence (AI), understand it, and make use of it. Yet AI is a mathematical science with machine learning at its heart, and the challenge is opening up that maths so AI outputs can be understood and trusted by people with any level of numeracy. That is what drives Xperfecta: building mathematical models that are psychologically intuitive, where the calculations lead to explanations that can be judged as valid or not, irrespective of the number-crunching beneath.

Putting you at the centre of your decisions

Xperfecta is a disruptive approach to decision making that empowers people to work together because they understand the reasons behind each outcome. It enables AI to work for people, not direct them. Based in North Devon but delivered through cloud services, it connects you to expertise from anywhere, at any time.

What we do

Xperfecta provides real-time evaluation of decision making, driven by a psychological model of human knowledge and reasoning. It specifies the most appropriate information to collect, identifies relevant hypotheses, evaluates evolving support using human expertise, and suggests intelligent formulations that link hypotheses to plans. It integrates decisions with current and historical data and provides intuitive explanations of how machine-learning outputs generate decision advice.
